Feature 8

Gated Community

Stepping Into Society Hill

Stepping into Society hill feels as if going into a completely different world. Not to mention, I could never have expected to find such a place hidden behind a grim mall, or when considering the path to it is filled with litter. Or even when taking into account that is surrounded by a large stretch of barren land, that is next to a very loud six lane traffic route.

Society hill represents the upper middle class. After walking into the gated community, the constant jabbing of car engines and horns suddenly fades away in the distance and we enter a neatly landscaped area with rows upon rows of neatly designed owned family homes. The homes come in a variety of designs with some looking more modern than others. Most homes of the homes have a brown brick or delicate colors. This implies that the development may have been ongoing for a period, and new designs were incorporated as time went on.

Compared to the national average of homes in the US, the homes listed as available, sit within a comfortable range of the $418,600 U.S. average of 2021 Q1(FRED).

The land on Society Hill has a long history dating back to the 1920s when the idea of an airport was originally proposed but was overturned by building the Roosevelt stadium. The stadium was iconic as it held momentous baseball games and concerts but became desolate in the 1970s after falling into disuse. Then in the 1990s the condominium we know today was built with the after sealing off the chromium contaminated land. Hence, making the homes relatively new, at about 30 years of age. The condominium was named and built in resemblance to the prestigious society hill in Philadelphia

To ask if society hill integrates well into the surrounding community, is the wrong question. Instead, a more appropriate one is how secluded Society Hill is from its neighborhood? Considering that unless you are looking for it, it is difficult to find, and the difference in wealth is more than apparent, this gated community is extremely secluded. As mentioned, it is tucked away neatly behind the unassuming slabs of concrete(mall) that guard it from any foreign outsiders. In addition, the entrance is guarded, blocking anyone who does not live there from accessing. On top of that patrol cars of JCPD can be seen doing rounds from time to time.
