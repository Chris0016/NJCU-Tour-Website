Culver Ave.

Traversing Culver Ave.

How Does the University Fit into This Neighborhood?

Overall, it can be said that the different buildings that belong to the university fit very well together with the environment. This is due in part to the buildings not being tall, and therefore not standing out and being out of place when compared to the other single-family homes that surround it. Moreover, the colors of buildings like the art building and the fitness center don’t stand out. Whether the university buildings standing out or not is good is a debatable topic, however, the building at least gives a sense that they belong where they currently are.

Is there landscaping here that can hold it together and create a pleasant ambience?

Landscaping is important to the scene because it provides a brighter and more vibrant environment which apparently lacks in the NJCU neighborhood. Take for instace the images below, which compare areas with and without landscaping.

Landscaping and looks is something that the area could really improve upon. The campus is filled with amazing landscapes that really shows in the spring season, but the surrounding area is a different story. Spots can be seen lacking any sort of greenery or having cracked sidewalks. In turn, this potrays a neighborhood that shows little care for their environment.Adding on, because NJCU is surrounded by an area that doesn't look good it downturns the value and outlook of the NJCU campus.
Another important point is there is no university presence around. To put it more simply, when stepping away from the campus, there are little indications that there is a college holding about 5,000 students. Some photos are shown below of Culver Ave. and Audobon Ave. to give some insight on the landscaping around NJCU.

Although it is difficult to quantify, the difference in effect and the sense that it gives it obvious at first glance.

As expected, since it is a residential area there is very little foot traffic in the area. Nevertheless, we can expect this to change in the upcoming years with the advent of more luxury apartments which will inevitably force the surrounding area to better accommodate those people with destination areas.
