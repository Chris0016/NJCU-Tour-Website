About this project

The area of Jersey City can be described as a city in on the border of explosive growth coupled with areas that show signs of neglect and decay. How do these pieces fit together? This project will look at the vicinity surrounding NJCU and explore nine locations from several different perspectives, with the most important being that of a city planner. The goal of this project is not to change Jersey City, but rather taking looking at a representative case study and asking essential questions that will provide a better analysis and criticism of the space.

Some of the questions asked include: How does the previous foundational structure affect what it is today? How do different structures fit in with the environment? What impression does this feature make? Moreover, this project also aims to analyze the space from a scientific view, by considering statistical data about the people seen in the area.
